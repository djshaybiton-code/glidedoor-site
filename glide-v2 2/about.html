Who We Are

Glide Door Experts LLC has been fixing sliding doors across the Phoenix East Valley since 2015. We're owner-operated — meaning the person who answers the phone is closely connected to the person doing the work. That's not common anymore, and it's something we're proud of.

We started with a simple observation: most homeowners don't know who to call when a sliding door breaks. The big home warranty companies are slow and expensive. The general handymen don't carry the right parts. And door replacement companies have a financial incentive to push replacement when repair is the right answer.

Glide Door Experts was built to be the right answer — fast, honest, correctly-equipped specialists who fix sliding doors and nothing else.

What We Do — and What We Don't

We specialize exclusively in sliding doors. Roller replacement, track repair and replacement, glass replacement, lock and handle repair, screen door service, and emergency repair. That's it.

Specialization means we're faster, better-equipped, and more experienced than a general handyman — and we don't need to charge for the learning curve. We've seen every sliding door problem in Arizona. We know what fails here and why, and we stock the right parts for Arizona's conditions.

We don't do windows. We don't do general carpentry. We don't install shower doors. Sliding doors only — done right.

Our Service Commitment

What We Always Do

  • Diagnose over the phone before arriving — free
  • Quote before touching anything
  • Carry parts on the truck for same-day repair

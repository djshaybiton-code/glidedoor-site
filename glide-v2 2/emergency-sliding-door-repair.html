What Counts as a Sliding Door Emergency?

Door completely off track — panel hanging or leaning, won't slide at all. Common after furniture impact or if roller fails completely.

Door won't close or lock — security risk. Can't leave the home safely or secure it for the night.

Shattered or cracked glass — safety hazard and complete exposure to elements.

Door stuck open in Arizona summer — your AC is fighting the outdoor heat directly. Every hour matters.

Rental or commercial property — tenant can't access or secure their unit. Liability issue for the property owner.

Why Gilbert Sliding Doors Fail Faster

  • ✓
    Rapid growth — newer hardware
    Most Gilbert doors are builder-grade from 2000s–2020s. These systems look fine but wear out within 8–12 years under Arizona conditions.
  • ✓
    High-use family homes
    Gilbert's family demographic means high door traffic — kids, pets, pool access multiple times daily — accelerating roller wear.
  • ✓
    HOA communities require fast repair
    Many Gilbert HOAs require prompt maintenance. A door that won't close properly is a violation waiting to happen.
  • ✓
    Desert expansion & contraction
    Gilbert's temperature swings cause metal frames to expand and contract, slowly knocking alignment out over time.
